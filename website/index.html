crts - a real time simulation game


crts is a real time simulation game. You control a group of workers by giving them tasks like "move" or "build". The world is procedurally generated as you explore it, and slowly changes over time as e.g. trees grow. Other features include online multiplayer and two user interfaces, a graphical one (opengl) and a text-based one (ncurses).

One of the main design principles is simplicity. In cases where I could avoid using a library by implementing a simpler solution, that is what I did. As a result, there are few dependencies. ncurses is required for the text-based ui, and glfw is required for the opengl ui (glfw may pull in additional dependencies depending on your platform).
