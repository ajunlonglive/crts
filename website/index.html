A handmade arcade rts
Use your mouse to control a swarm of cubes. Right-click to consume terrain and enemy cubes and grow your swarm. Left-click to sacrifice your cubes and create terrain.
Features:
  • handmade from the ground up
  • low resource usage
  • cool terrain generator
  • fully customizeable vim-like keymap system
  • text-mode (curses) ui
